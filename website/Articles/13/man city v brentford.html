Match Preview:Brentford Vs Man City; Premier League

Manchester City are back in action following a quick turn-around at the Gtech Community Stadium.

Pep Guardiola’s men are fresh off a comfortable 3-1 win vs Vincent Kompany’s Burnley on Wednesday night at the Etihad Stadium.

Birthday boy Julian Alvarez had a brace and Rodri curled one home in typical fashion to make the game before half a minute elapsed in the second-half.

Brentford come in having lost four of their last five in league play, most recently a 3-2 defeat to cross-city opponents Tottenham.

Head-to-head

Brentford were stunningly the only team in the Premier League to take all six points from City last season. The Bees stung late in a 1-2 result at the Etihad Stadium thanks to an Ivan Toney double in the last match before the World Cup. The return fixture was a 1-0 win for Brentford in the final league assignment of 2022/23.

Where to Watch

Fans in the UK can watch the game on Sky Sports Main Event, Sky Sports Premier League and Sky Sports Ultra HDR.

Team News

Sergio Reguilon was out for Brentford’s loss to Tottenham due to the loan rules in place, but could make a return to the squad in time for Monday's encounter.

Pep Guardiola is expecting a fully fit squad available for the first time since August.

Score Prediction

While the Bees won home and away against City last season, the West London outfit have one win in their last six. The Blues have started their charge for a potential fourth successive Premier League title and have returned to full fitness.

Brentford 1-3 Manchester City.
