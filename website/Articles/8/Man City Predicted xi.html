Manchester City vs Huddersfield Town, Predicted XI, #EmiratesFACup Third Round

Manchester City are set to begin the defence of their Emirates FA Cup crown in the third-round vs Championship opposition Huddersfield Town.

With Pep Guardiola announcing news on nine players in his preview of the game, here's how we think the Blues will start their clash with The Terriers:

Pep Guardiola is known to rotate his goalkeepers for domestic cup matches. Stefan Ortega started all Carabao Cup and FA Cup fixtures last season, and we expect him to get the nod between the sticks.

Kyle Walker has been a mainstay in the team this season, and missed only one FA Cup match last season. It's likely the experience full-back continues to be a regular for this clash.

With John Stones out due to injury, Manuel Akanji will more than likely have his chance to shine.

Nathan Ake will be expected to partner the Swiss international in defence.

Cup ties like this are the perfect opportunities for young players, and Rico Lewis - while young - is also quickly becoming a regular member for the Blues.

With news of Kalvin Phillips being sick, and the need to give Rodri rest for more important games ahead, Mateo Kovacic will more than likely be slotted into a defensive midfield role.

Bernardo Silva is our pick to start next to the Croatian international.
Phil Foden has been the difference maker for City of late, and we think he'll be picked to build on his outstanding form.

As aforementioned, young players often get a perfect opportunity to shine in cup ties like these. Oscar Bobb has been praised repeatedly by Pep Guardiola, so we expect he will get a chance to earn more here.

Erling Haaland has just come back to training with the full squad, so we expect Pep Guardiola to start Julian Alvarez instead of risking the Norwegian.
