Player By Player: Here Is How We Rated The World Champions' Performance Against Sheffield United

Here are our Player Ratings from a comfortable evening at an Etihad Stadium that saw no visitors able to win throughout 2023.

Ederson - 7: Had one save to make in the game; flawless distribution again.

Josko Gvardiol - 6.5: Not the best decision-making when on the ball, but recovered well each time.

Nathan Ake - 7.5: A standardly great performance, as he mopped up whenever Sheffield United looked for their long ball in-behind.

Manuel Akanji - 7.5: Showcased great ability on the ball, which will be necessary with John Stones out with injury.

Kyle Walker - 6.5: Average performance; some shots from 30+ yards that never looked threatening.

Rodri - 8.5: Well taken goal; dictated play in the middle like how we've been used to.

Mateo Kovacic - 6: Sloppy on the ball a few times, one of which leading to his booking in the first-half.

Phil Foden - 9.5 [MOTM]: *Another* outstanding display; constantly threatened on the ball; assisted both goals.

Jack Grealish - 6.5: Understandably average performance, given recent personal events; limped off when substituted, hopefully not an injury.

Julian Alvarez - 8: Great pressing from the Argentine; scored a goal and looked great on the ball.

Bernardo Silva - 6.5: Didn't do much; could've taken his chance better in the first-half.

Subsitutes:

Oscar Bobb - 7: Fantastic cameo; helped setup the second goal; looked good on the ball.

Rico Lewis - 6.5: Brought more control once he replaced Mateo Kovacic.

Ruben Dias - 6.5: Been dropped recently, but this performance was better than a lot of his recent performances.
